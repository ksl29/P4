Jojo's Bizarre Adventure: Battle Tendency

Summary

Taking place in 1938-39, the story follows the misadventures of Joseph Joestar (a.k.a. JoJo), grandson of Jonathan, as he masters his innate Ripple abilities in order to combat hostile, ancient super-beings named the Pillar Men, creators of the Stone Mask that plot to become the ultimate lifeforms. Along the way, Joseph trains with other warriors of the Ripple.
